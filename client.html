✉ Introduction

This application tries to assist players in analyzing their performance and playstyles. It provides summaries for summoners, champions and items that occured in a player's match history. Contrary to many other websites it does not display statistics for whole regions or leagues, but instead focuses on a player.

Note that results only display summoners, champions and items that occured at least a certain number of times (based on the number of total matches analyzed). This helps reduce clutter - especially for allied and enemy summoners. Games that ended in a /remake are not added to summaries. Item results count every single item purchase that was not undone. Wins shown are wins for the user you specify. That means the "Win" row on enemy champions shows how many of these champions were beaten.

Score Calculation

Scores shown are meant to give an idea about a player's performance with a champion or item compared to each other. Basically they are calculated similar to ratios, but with a little twist:

  • If wins > losses, then the score = (wins+1)/(losses+2)
  • If losses > wins, then the score = -(losses+1)/(wins+2)
  • In case wins ans losses are equal, the score shows 0

This formula ensures that 200 wins vs 100 losses score higher than 2 wins vs 1 losses. A sample size of 300 is much more reliable than one of 3. Scores close to 0 indicate either balance or a lack of certainty.

Do note that this score does not necessarily show the power of an item. Control wards for example will usually need to be replaced much more frequently by teams that do not dominate their enemy. As such they could easily get a negative score. The score you see merely suggests that something might be worth looking into.
